I am NOT

The message of I am NOT is about identity crisis where one does not know who they are. Stray Kids used this album to represent people who are struggling and to tell them that they are not alone in that battle they're facing.

Tracklist: (7/7)

I am WHO

I am WHO is about the journey in finding your own self. As a sequel to "I am NOT", it's message is like asking 'If this is not me, then who am I?'

Tracklist: (7/7)

I am YOU

I am YOU is basically the conclusion for the I am Series. It's about finding yourself (I) through or in someone who is always by your side (YOU). Just like from the lyrics in the title track, "I am YOU, I see me in YOU."

Tracklist: (7/7)
